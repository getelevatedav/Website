What We Do

Every detail, considered.

From dedicated home cinemas to seamless whole-home networks — we design, install, and support systems that just work.

🎬

Home Theater

Dedicated cinema rooms with 4K projection, immersive Dolby Atmos sound, acoustic treatment, and lighting control. From cozy media rooms to full-scale private theaters.

📺

TV Mounting & Installation

Professional flat-panel mounting on any surface — drywall, brick, stone, or above the mantel. Always with clean wire concealment and proper cable management.

🔊

Surround Sound

5.1, 7.1, Atmos — matched to your room's acoustics. In-wall, in-ceiling, or freestanding speaker systems calibrated for reference-quality sound.

🎵

Whole-Home Audio

Music in every room, on demand. Sonos, Bowers & Wilkins, Bluesound, and more — multi-zone audio that's simple to control from your phone.

🔒

Security Systems

HD camera systems, smart doorbells, motion detection, and remote monitoring. See your home from anywhere with professionally installed security.

📡

Network & WiFi

Enterprise-grade networking for your home. Structured wiring, managed switches, wireless access points — no dead zones, no buffering, no excuses.

💡

Lighting Control

Smart lighting scenes, dimmers, and keypads that set the perfect mood. Lutron, Ketra, and other premium systems designed around how you live.

🏠

Smart Home Automation

One-touch control of your entire home. Lights, shades, audio, climate, security — all unified through intuitive interfaces you'll actually enjoy using.

🪟

Motorized Shades

Automated window treatments that respond to time of day, sunlight, or a single tap. Clean installations with hidden hardware and whisper-quiet motors.

🌿

Outdoor Audio & Video

Weather-rated speakers, outdoor displays, and landscape audio for patios, pools, and outdoor living spaces that sound as good as they look.

🔌

Low Voltage Wiring

Clean, code-compliant structured wiring for new construction and retrofits. HDMI, Cat6, speaker wire, coax — all run with precision and labeled clearly.

🏗️

Pre-Wire & New Construction

Partner with us during the build phase. We work with your builder and architect to future-proof your home with the right infrastructure from day one.
